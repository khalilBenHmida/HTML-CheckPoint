Resident Evil 4 is a critically acclaimed survival horror video game developed and published by Capcom.
It was originally released for the Nintendo GameCube in 2005 and later for various other platforms, including PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, PC, Wii, and more.
The game is the sixth installment in the Resident Evil series and marked a significant departure from its predecessors in terms of gameplay mechanics.
Unlike the fixed camera angles and pre-rendered backgrounds of earlier Resident Evil games, Resident Evil 4 adopted an over-the-shoulder, third-person perspective.
This change allowed for more dynamic and action-oriented gameplay.
